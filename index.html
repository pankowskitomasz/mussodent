Cosmetic and family dentistry

Your top-rated dental practice

At MussoDent we take the time to help our patients enjoy their dental visits. From when you arrive to when you leave, our staff will help you enjoy your visit. Our stylish practice also has an urban loft feel that we think you'll love.

When you first walk in, you are given an Ipad for efficient, accurate and confidential gathering of information. We strive to have the best equipment and latest technology dentistry has to offer. This allows our team to not only communicate dental needs with our patients, but to also provide dentistry in the most efficient and comfortable way possible.

Combine this with an amazingly friendly staff and you can see why so many people love our team. We are both appreciative and grateful for the hundreds of five star reviews our current patients have shared. With views overlooking Market Street Square, we provide a unique and relaxing environment for your entire family.

Dentistry for the entire family

From customized cleanings to cosmetics, we can help.

Our team can handle the dental needs of your entire family. Do you need a dentist that can help you or your children overcome dental anxiety?

We want each child to have a positive dental experience in our office so they can foster a fearless relationship with dentistry throughout their life. Do you have a complicated dental procedure you need resolved?

With the latest dental technologies at our location and a team of dentists experienced in complex dental procedures, our team will help you understand the different options available to you for the best results. Dr. Musso and her team can help you and your family achieve a healthy and beautiful smile.

Experience the difference

Every new patient will experience our comprehensive dental exam. This includes a detailed cleaning, oral cancer screening, and intraoral digital photos will be taken so you can clearly see any dental problems. With our digital intraoral photography, you will be able to view your teeth and any concerns you have on our large flat screen monitors equipped in each room.

Educating our patients is of utmost importance; as the patient, you will always select the treatment recommendation you prefer. We promise our thorough evaluation will exceed your expectations.

Comprehensive care

With our doctors' extensive training, we provide most dental procedures right here in our office. From dental implants, root canals, wisdom tooth extractions, and more, our surgical dentists can often complete these procedures in one appointment for the convenience of our patients.

From having your crown completed in one day with our CEREC system to providing specialized services such as all levels of sedation dentistry, our broad range of in-house services mean you don't have to worry about being referred elsewhere.

Dr. Musso

Dr. Musso is committed to focusing on each patients specific needs.
