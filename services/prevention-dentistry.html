Prevention services

PREVENTION IS OF THE UTMOST IMPORTANCE TO THIS PRACTICE.

We believe keeping your own teeth healthy is the top priority. Finding decay early can often eliminate the need for extensive dental work. Our practice has highly educated dental hygienists that provide thorough cleanings, sealants, oral cancer screenings, and periodontal therapy when needed.

We utilize the best dental materials and equipment available today, including a dental x-ray system that has the lowest amount of radiation exposure on the market. Read more about our prevention services below.

  • Custom Cleanings
    Regular exams are an important part of maintaining your oral health.
  • Fluoride Treatments
    Fluoride treatment in our office takes just a few minutes.
  • Sealants
    Sometimes brushing is not enough, especially when it comes to hard-to-reach spots in your mouth.
  • Oral Cancer Screenings
    Our office is not only dedicated to your smile, we're also dedicated to your overall wellness.
  • Gum Disease
    Protect yourself from one of the top reasons for tooth loss in adults.
  • Oral Appliances
    Protect your teeth from grinding with convenient mouth guards.
