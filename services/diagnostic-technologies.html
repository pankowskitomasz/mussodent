Diagnostic technologies

Our office uses state of the art technology in order to ensure that patients are receiving a quality experience. Read more about our technology services below.

  • Digital X-rays
    We use handheld x-ray devices, meaning patients don't have to move during the x-ray, saving them time during their appointment.
  • 3D Cone Beam X-ray
    Our 3D cone beam x-ray produces less radiation than regular x-rays, allowing for a safer environment.
  • CEREC Machine
    These cameras help our team get a closer look at your oral health for a more accurate diagnosis.
