Dr. Kate Musso

Dr. Musso graduated from Texas A&M University with a bachelor’s degree in Biology. She continued her education at the University of Texas Dental School in Houston, graduating with honors in 2004.

Dr. Musso received her Fellowship award in the Academy of General Dentistry in 2012, recognizing her passion for clinical excellence through hundreds of hours of continuing education.

She has attended some of the most elite post-doctoral programs in the country, including the Dr. John Kerry, Pankey Institutes, and Dr. Michael Shear. Dr. Musso is passionate about delivering the finest level of dental care to her patients.

DR. Michael Loom

Dr. Loom joined Dr. Musso's dental team in the summer of 2015.

Dr. Loom graduated from Texas A&M University with a bachelor’s degree in Biomedical Sciences. He continued his dental education at UT Health Science Center San Antonio, graduating with honors in 1994. He completed his post-doctorate residency program at UTHSCSA where he focused on advanced dental techniques including: removal of wisdom teeth, implant dentistry, IV sedation (sleep dentistry), and complex patient management.

Dr. Loom is committed to comprehensive patient care through advanced dental techniques.

Dr. Matthew Gump

Dr. Gump joined Dr. Musso and her team in the spring of 2019.

Dr. Gump graduated from the University of Kentucky with a bachelor's degree in Biology. He then continued his dental degree at University of Louisville, School of Dentistry, where he graduated with honors in 1996.

Dr. Gump focuses on oral surgery, implants, cosmetic and structural dentistry. Dr. Gump is dedicated to delivering the highest quality dental care while maintaining patient comfort as his top priority.

Dr. Sandra Lines

Dr. Lines graduated with honors from Roanoke College with a bachelor’s degree in Chemistry and a concentration in Health Care Delivery. She went on to obtain her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from Virginia Commonwealth University in Richmond, Virginia. From there, she continued into an Advanced Education in General Dentistry residency program at UT Health in Houston where she focused on caring for patients with complex dental needs including full mouth reconstruction, cosmetics, and restorative dentistry.

Dr. Lines has a strong artistic background and is committed to improving the appearance of each patient's smile while maintaining optimal functionality.
